Enterprise Copy Data Analytics (eCDA) is a cloud analytics product offered by Dell EMC designed to provide a global view into the effectiveness of a user's data protection operations and infrastructure using predictive and perscriptive analytics. eCDA provides features such as a 0-100 health score, capacity forecasting for data protection hardware, and recommendations to improve the resiliency of a user's data protection environment.

While working on eCDA, I served as a technical liason between product management, engineering, marketing, and the CTO office. I helped establish design thinking and lean UX principles by pushing for quicker design iterations and frequent customer communication. I developed test plans and tested product features with customers as well as internal stakeholders. Through those tests, I collected qualitative and quantitative data and helped integrate that feedback into the product. Metrics such as Net Promotor Score, Customer Satisfaction, and Ease of Use served as benchmarks for improvement. I also participated in feature reviews, worked closely with product management to prioritize features based on customer feedback and survey responses, and integrated passive in-product usage analytics to better understand popular and critical features and workflows.
